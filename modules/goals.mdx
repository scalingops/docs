## Overview

The Goals module helps you transform client feedback and business objectives into actionable, trackable goals. Create goals from survey responses, set priorities, and monitor progress toward achieving strategic objectives.

## Creating Goals

### Manual Goal Creation
<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Goal">
    Access the goal creation form from the Goals page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Goal Details">
    - Title and description
    - Category selection
    - Priority level (High, Medium, Low)
    - Target completion date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Ownership">
    Select responsible team member or department
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Success Metrics">
    Define measurable success criteria
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Goal">
    Goal appears in your goals list and planner
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating Goals from Surveys
Goals can be automatically created from survey responses:
1. Navigate to Surveys module
2. Review survey responses
3. Select feedback requiring action
4. Click "Create Goal from Feedback"
5. Goal is pre-populated with context from survey

## Goal Properties

### Essential Information
- **Title**: Clear, actionable goal statement
- **Description**: Detailed context and requirements
- **Category**: Organizational grouping
- **Priority**: High, Medium, or Low
- **Status**: Planning, In Progress, Completed, On Hold
- **Due Date**: Target completion date

### Tracking Fields
- **Progress Percentage**: 0-100% completion
- **Milestones**: Key checkpoints
- **Notes**: Updates and comments
- **Attachments**: Related documents

## Goal Categories

Organize goals by category:
- **Service Improvement**: Enhance service delivery
- **Client Satisfaction**: Address feedback and concerns
- **Operational Excellence**: Internal improvements
- **Growth**: Business development initiatives
- **Compliance**: Regulatory and framework goals
- **Custom Categories**: Create your own categories

### Linking to Planner
Goals automatically appear in the Planner module:

### Creating Planner Items

4. Link maintains relationship
