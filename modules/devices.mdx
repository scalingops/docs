## Overview

The Devices module provides comprehensive asset management for client hardware. Track devices throughout their lifecycle, manage warranties, plan refreshes, and maintain accurate inventory across all client organizations.

## Device Management

### Device Information
Track comprehensive device details:
- Device name and identifier
- Type and model
- Serial number
- Asset tag
- Purchase date
- Warranty information
- Assigned user
- Location
- Status

### Device Types
Manage various device categories:
- **Workstations**: Desktops and laptops
- **Servers**: Physical and virtual servers
- **Network Equipment**: Routers, switches, firewalls
- **Mobile Devices**: Phones and tablets
- **Printers**: Printers and multifunction devices
- **Peripherals**: Monitors, keyboards, accessories
- **Custom Types**: Define your own categories
