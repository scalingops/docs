## Creating & Managing Items

### Quick Add
Create new planner items directly from the interface:
1. Click the **+ New Item** button
2. Select item type (Goal, Compliance, Device, Project, Opportunity)
3. Fill in details (title, dates, assignee, priority)
4. Save to add to timeline

### Item Properties
- **Title & Description**: Clear identification and details
- **Date Range**: Start and end dates for timeline placement
- **Assignment**: Responsible person or team
- **Priority**: Critical, High, Medium, Low
- **Company**: Associated client company
- **Status**: Track progress and completion

### Drag & Drop
Easily reschedule items by dragging them on the timeline:
- Click and hold any item
- Drag to new date
- Release to update schedule
- Changes save automatically
