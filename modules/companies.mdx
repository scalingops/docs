## Overview

The Companies module is the foundation for organizing your client base in MSPortal.ai. It provides comprehensive tools for managing client information, organizational structures, and service relationships.

## Company Management

### Company Information
<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Details">
    - Company name and legal entity
    - Industry and company size
    - Tax ID and registration numbers
    - Primary location and addresses
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service Information">
    - Service tier and package
    - Contract start and end dates
    - Billing information
    - Support hours and response times
  </Step>
  <Step title="Technical Details">
    - Network configuration
    - Domain information
    - Security requirements
    - Compliance standards
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Organizational Structure

<Info>
  MSPortal.ai supports multi-level organizational hierarchies for complex client structures
</Info>

#### Hierarchy Levels
- **Parent Company**: Top-level organization
- **Subsidiaries**: Child companies under parent
- **Departments**: Organizational units within companies
- **Locations**: Physical or logical locations
