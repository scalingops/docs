1\. MSPortal.ai comes with a lot of built in checks that you can utilize from CIS. The center for Internet security. You can add these to your tenant by clicking settings.\
Playbooks\
Then searching or clicking on this page. You can easily select multiple or only one check.

![](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-08-29/206f39ba-2c12-4fae-8fd6-96ae34c0a168/stack_animation.webp)
